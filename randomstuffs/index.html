Recommendations

For a safer on-line browsing experience


FireFox Browser

Firefox is the highly secure web browser which allows for add-ons of extra protection.

HTTPS Eve.

HTTPS Everywhere encrypts your communications, making your browsing more secure.

CCleaner

CCleaner is a tool for cleaning your PC Registry and Browser History. Get rid of those cookies!


Ghostery

Ghostery protect your privacy by seeing who's tracking your web browsing and blocking them.

Cryptostorm

Cryptostorm VPN is a secure token-based on-line security & privacy service.


Adblock Plus

Adblock Plus blocks annoying ads, tracking, malware and others.


Technitium MAC

Technitium MAC allows you to change MAC Address on your (NIC) or (WiFi) cards.


TOR Project

Tor is free proxy service that helps you defend against traffic analysis & surveillance on-line.
